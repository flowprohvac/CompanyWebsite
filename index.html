Frequently Asked Questions

The questions we get most often from new customers in Corona and nearby cities.

What HVAC brands do you install?

We install top American brands — Carrier, Trane, Lennox, American Standard, and Rheem. We recommend the right brand and efficiency tier for your home and budget rather than pushing one brand on every customer.

Do you offer a warranty on new installations?

Yes. Every new HVAC installation comes with a 10-year manufacturer parts warranty when properly registered — and we handle the registration for you on installation day. Coverage typically includes the compressor (or heat exchanger), coils, and major internal components. Labor warranty terms are separate.

Will you beat another HVAC company's price?

Yes. Bring us a written, itemized estimate from any licensed California HVAC contractor for the same equipment and scope, and we'll beat it. We won't quietly downgrade equipment to win on price — same brand, same scope, lower number.

Do you offer free HVAC estimates in Corona?

Yes. We provide free, no-obligation estimates for new HVAC installations and major repairs throughout Corona and surrounding Inland Empire cities. For diagnostic visits on a broken system, we charge a small service-call fee that gets credited toward the repair if you move forward with us.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. FlowProHVAC is a licensed (CSLB #1142668), bonded, and insured HVAC contractor in California with more than 30 years of hands-on field experience. We carry general liability and workers' compensation coverage so your home and our team are protected on every job.

Can you come out today?

Most days during cooling and heating seasons, yes — especially if you call early. We prioritize urgent calls and most repairs are completed the same day. Our hours are Monday through Saturday, 7 AM to 7 PM.

How much does a new AC system cost in Corona?

It depends on the size of your home, the SEER rating you choose, and whether your existing ductwork needs work. A typical single-family home in Corona will land somewhere in the $7,000 – $14,000 range for a complete replacement. We provide a detailed written estimate — and we'll beat any written competitor quote on equivalent equipment.

How often should I have my HVAC system serviced?

Twice a year — once before cooling season and once before heating season. Regular tune-ups extend equipment life, keep efficiency up, and catch small issues before they become expensive failures. Ask about our maintenance plan.
